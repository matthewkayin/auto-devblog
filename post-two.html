Get to Know Me

November 26, 2019

So who am I, anyways? Well, my name's Matt and I'm a software developer. I'm graduating with a degree in Computer Science soon, but I started programming way before that. I was drawn to programming at an early age because I like creativity and I like problem solving, and programming was something that combined the two. I love creating things with code and I love learning new languages or experimenting with new technologies.
