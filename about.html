About Me

Hi! My name is Matt. I'm an aspiring Software Developer and a Computer Science student entering my last semester at Wichita State University.

Although I've been studying engineering, I'm a creative person at heart, and I've long held that if it weren't for coding, I would have probably majored in English or something. Instead, I fell in love with the way software development brings together creativity and problem solving. It's like a math problem where you make something at the end, and I think that's really cool.

I created this blog as a way to share my passion with anybody who cares to come and see it. Here is where I post devlogs of my current software projects. I encourage you to check it out if you haven't already! And if you're interested in seeing my work beyond what's on the blog, you find my github here.
